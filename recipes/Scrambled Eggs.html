Scrambled Eggs

    • Scrambled Eggs in a Mug

    For delicious scrambled eggs, microwave one egg in a cup with a bit of milk and pepper for each person in your family. It's quick and easy, and there will be no messy pan to clean.

    Ingredients

    • 1 large egg
    • 1 tablespoon milk
    • 1 pinch ground black pepper (Optional)

    Steps

    1. Gather all ingredients.
    2. Crack egg into a microwave-safe mug; beat until even in color.
    3. Pour in milk and beat until light yellow in color.
    4. Microwave on high until cooked through and fluffy, about 90 seconds. Season with pepper and serve.
